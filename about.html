Who we are

Kitegomba Medical Center, We are a community driven healthcare provider serving Kasangati, Gayaza, and the surrounding areas. We are passionate about improving lives through patient centered care, health, education, and reliable ,edical servies tailored to meet the needs of every family.

Our Mission:

To provide accessible, affordable, and high quality healtcare services that promote wellness, prevent illness, and improve the quality of life for individuals and families in our community through compassionate and professional care.

Our Vision

To be the leading healthcare provider in Kasangati, Gayaza. recognised for excellence compassion, and innovation in patient care.
